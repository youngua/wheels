Wheels-gon

Wheels-round

Wheels-gon and wheels-round is a variable typeface experiment made as a project for Marie Otsuka 2020 Spring Web Type course at the Rhode Island School of Design.

It's form is inspired by the circle grid and the fact that "nothing in the physical world passes the perfect circle test, where every point on the circumference is exactly equidistant from the circle's center."

Free for non-commercial use. Attribute typeface to graceyoung.design
